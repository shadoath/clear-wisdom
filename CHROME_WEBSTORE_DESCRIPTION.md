DETAILED DESCRIPTION

Start every browsing session with wisdom.

Clear Wisdom brings James Clear's best insights, quotes, and questions directly to your new tab. Each time you open a new tab, you'll discover a gem from his wildly popular 3-2-1 newsletter.


THREE TYPES OF WISDOM

Ideas - James Clear's original insights on decision-making, habits, and living well (3 per newsletter)

Quotes - Curated wisdom from great thinkers throughout history (2 per newsletter)

Questions - Thought-provoking questions to spark reflection (1 per newsletter)


POWERFUL FEATURES

Search - Find that perfect quote you remember

Favorites - Save quotes that resonate with you

View Tracking - See how often you've encountered each piece of wisdom

Filter by Year - Browse wisdom from specific time periods

Category Toggle - Focus on Ideas, Quotes, or Questions

Refresh - Get a new insight anytime


EVER-GROWING COLLECTION

300+ newsletters curated and formatted

Updated monthly with fresh insights

Years of wisdom at your fingertips

All content from James Clear's official 3-2-1 newsletter


CLEAN AND MINIMAL

No clutter. No distractions. Just wisdom displayed beautifully in a clean, readable format that respects your browsing experience.


PERFECT FOR

Fans of James Clear and Atomic Habits

Anyone building better habits

People who love wisdom quotes

Those seeking daily inspiration

Readers of the 3-2-1 newsletter


HOW IT WORKS

1. Install the extension
2. Open a new tab
3. Receive wisdom

That's it! Every new tab becomes a moment of reflection and insight.
